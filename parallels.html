Parallels

As I have been getting more into machining, I've found the need for a good set of parallels for use when clamping work pieces in milling vices. I made them myself to gain get some more machining practice. The process started with 1/4 inch thick 6 inch wide cold rolled steel. I cut out pieces from 2 inches to 3/8th of an inch in 1/8th inch steps. I then squared each set of pieces with a fly-cutter and milled out a pattern on the CNC mill. They are pictured as they are just back from being case-hardened. I plan to have them blanchard ground and black-oxide treated before I grind the top and bottom edges.
