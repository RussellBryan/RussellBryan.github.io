ProCo Rat Clones

Some friends and I are fans of the band PWR BTTM. The distortion pedal used in most of thier songs is the ProCo Rat. Its a pretty simple circuit and I found I could build one for $40. Not only that, but I could get the original components (dated ineffiecent ICs) on ebay for a couple bucks. Pedals with the original componenets go for over $100 and suposedly sound much better. Two of my friends were interested in the pedals so I made a pair of them.
