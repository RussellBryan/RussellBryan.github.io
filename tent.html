Backpacking Tent

I wanted to build an ultra lite tent and this is the design I cam up with after studying a few on the Internet. The top is a catenary and its held up on both ends with trekking poles. I made it big enough to be very comfortable for one person and just big enough for two.

I got a pretty good deal on 40D rip-stop nylon but soon after found I could have bought 2nds quality sil-nylon from diygearsupply.com. All in all the tent cost me about $100 and weights just over 2 lbs. Compared to 4 lbs and $250 of a typical backpacking tent, I'm very happy with it.
