Backpacking Tent

I wanted to build an ultra lite tent and this is the design I cam up with after studying a few on the internet. The top is a catenary and its held up on both ends with treking poles. I made it big enough to be very confortable for one person and just big enough for two.

I got a pretty good deal on 40D ripstop nylon but soon after found I could have bought 2nds quality silnylon from diygearsupply.com. All in all the tent cost me about $100 and weights just over 2 lbs. Compared to 4 lbs and $250, I'm very happy with it.
