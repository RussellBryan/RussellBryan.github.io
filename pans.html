I made these two videos and compiled them into a gif to illustrate the difference between cast-iron and stainless steel aluminum tri-ply pans. I covered the pans with powdered sugar so that the temperature of the pan could be visualized as it heats up. The first thing to notice is that the area the heat is conducted into the pan is actually much smaller that the size of the hot plate. Next is the temperature gradient across the pan. The aluminum layer in the stainless steel pan spreads the heat evenly throughout the pan.
